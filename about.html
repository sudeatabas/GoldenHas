About

Welcome to GoldenHas, the magazine was established in 2023 by Lara Naz Çayır and Dila Naz Öğünç with a curiosity about how we could push the boundaries of the university. From day one, GoldenHas's mission has been to create a groundbreaking platform where creativity and freedom of expression develop without any limitations or prejudice. Dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of journalistic integrity and inclusivity, we aim to give voice to diverse perspectives and stories within our community. Our vision is to revolutionize university media by empowering students to explore, research, and narrate their truths with complete autonomy. By harnessing the collective power of student journalists, artists, and thinkers, we are committed to transforming the landscape of university journalism. Through our magazine, we strive to challenge the status quo by fostering an environment where every student feels represented and heard. Our commitment to impartial reporting and unrestricted expression stands as a testament to the revolutionary spirit that drives our publication forward, making us a symbol of innovation and change in academia and beyond.
